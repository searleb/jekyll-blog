Home Sweet Home was born out of the "boss mans" desire to have a central dashboard for employees at the MF studio.

With this in mind I decided it was a perfect project to learn the full stack javascript framework, Meteor.

This is what it has so far...

  • Company restricted Google login
  • Google Calendar day overview
  • Web notifications for up coming calendar events
  • Timesheets view
  • Search Google Drive and Wiki
  • Company Medium feed
  • LDN/SYD studio time, date and weather
  • Categorised tools list
  • Meeting rooms availability with progress indicator
  • Open jobs view
  • Internal podcast player
  • User settings
  • Sidebar nav

API's & data:

  • Google Calendar
  • Robin Rooms
  • WorkflowMax
  • Google Sheets
  • Medium RSS
